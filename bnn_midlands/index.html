Avery Bancroft Arrested

At approximately 2PM on April 19, 2015, the Midlands State Police Department arrested two people: Avery Bancroft, a prominent entertainment mogul in our state, and Chase Covington, the chairperson of the Midlands Gambling Commission. Preliminary reports indicate that the arrest is linked to the Midlands Gambling Commissions's vote to provide Bancroft with a license to operate a second casino in Midlands on Friday, April 17, 2015. A source inside the MSPD indicates that the arrest is the result of a long-term sting operation aimed at reducing the rampant corruption in Midlands government. BNN, as always, is on the scene. Check back right here each month for updates on this developing story.

    License Revoked

    Dr. Campbell Solo, the incoming Chair of the Midlands Board of Psychology announced today that it has preemptively revoked the license of Logan Bashir, PhD., J.D. from practicing in the state. Solo said: "I know that Dr. Bashir was never actually licensed to practice in our state, but, I saw Logan testify in a trial, and, frankly, it was embarassing. So, I'm making my first action as Chair of the Midlands Board of Psychology a preemptive ban on Dr. Bashir's ability to practice in this state ever again. I don't know if I can actually do this, but I'm doing it anyway."
