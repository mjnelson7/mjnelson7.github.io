New Details in Bancroft-Covington Case

BNN has exclusive new details regarding the alleged corruption scandal currently unfolding in Midlands. Recall that last month, BNN was first to bring you the report that a grand jury indicted both Chase Covington and Avery Bancroft on bribery charges. Bancroft allegedly bribed Covington in order to obtain a casino license.

BNN sat down with Avery Bancroft for an exclusive interview. Speaking out for the first time, Avery Bancroft told BNN reporter Reagan Thomas "This whole thing was a set-up. I was basically the police's puppet."

BNN reached out to Chase Covington’s attorneys. They declined to comment.

BNN, as always, is on the scene. Check back right here for updates on this developing story. We'll have updates at least monthly before all is revealed in mid-August.
