  Welcome! I'm an Assistant Professor in the Department of Political Science at The Pennsylvania State University. I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Political Science at Washington University in St. Louis where I was also a Graduate Student Associate in the Center for Empirical Research in the Law.

I study and teach about American political institutions, with particular attention to state judicial politics and public evaluations of judicial institutions. Before coming to Washington University, I received a B.A. in Political Science and English with a concentration in Business Law from Drake University in Des Moines, Iowa.

My research has been published or is forthcoming in the American Journal of Political Science, The Journal of Law and Courts, Political Research Quarterly, State Politics & Policy Quarterly, Judicature, and Electoral Studies.
