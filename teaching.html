Teaching

In Spring 2012, I taught American Politics in University College at Washington University in St. Louis. I also cotaught (with Alicia Uribe) Introduction to Quantitative Methods in University College in Spring 2013. I am currently designing an upper-division undergraduate course on recent decisions of the United States Supreme Court which I will offer in January, 2014. Outside of the classroom, I coach Washington University's undergraduate mock trial team.

For my work as a teaching assistant and student mentor at Washington University, I have been awarded the Dean's Award for Teaching Excellence.

I have served as a teaching assistant for the following courses:
  • Energy & the Environment (Professor Bill Lowry, Spring 2012)
  • American Politics (Professor Bill Lowry, Fall 2010 and 2011)
  • Defendants' Rights (Professor Jim Spriggs, Spring 2011)
  • The American Political System (Professor Joanna Mosser, Fall 2008, Drake University)

For more information on my teaching philosophy or to request copies of my teaching evaluations, please contact me.
